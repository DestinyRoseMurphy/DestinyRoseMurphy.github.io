Data Analysis

There is a nearly unimaginable amount of research that can be conducted on only the relatively small amount of data gathered for this project. Below, four crimes (misdemeanor marijuana possession, misdemeanor second DWI offenses, felony murder, and felony family violence) have been selected for special extended statistical analysis. Aside from these statistical findings, this project has uncovered a great deal of information regarding the level of political transparency common to Texas District Attorney offices.
